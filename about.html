Our Story

The Department of Chemistry offers an M.Sc. Chemistry program at VIT-AP University. This program provides ample opportunities for students to choose courses from a broad spectrum of cutting-edge areas in addition to fundamentals of organic, inorganic, physical, analytical, and computational chemistry.

Sessions on improving technical skills, scientific writing, oral presentations, and research expertise have been incorporated into the curriculum. Furthermore, the students will have the scope to pursue their post-graduation thesis work at various national and international institutions and industries.

Scholarships

100% scholarship opportunity for M.Sc. Chemistry students

  • G.V. PG Merit Scholarship: 100% Full Merit scholarship to a candidate who has secured CGPA of 9.0 or 90% and above in the qualifying UG examination.
  • Rajeswari PG Merit Scholarship: 50% scholarship to a candidate who has secured CGPA of 8.0 or 80% and above, and an additional 25% for a girl candidate making it a 75% scholarship.
